Privacy Policy

Updated 12-08-2025

WellCure values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you interact with our website, fill out forms, or use our offers.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, location, and details you provide in our forms. We also collect non-personal information such as browser type, device type, IP address, and website usage data. Offer-related data may also be collected, including health-related preferences you share with us.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide relevant healthcare offers and recommendations, communicate updates and promotions, improve our services, and comply with legal requirements.

3. Data Sharing

We may share your details with trusted third-party healthcare providers to fulfill your chosen offer. We do not sell or rent your personal information to unaffiliated parties. Third parties are required to handle your data in compliance with applicable privacy laws.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience and track offer performance. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect site functionality.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your data from unauthorized access. While we strive for high security, no method of data transmission over the internet is completely secure.

6. Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us. You may opt out of promotional communications at any time.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically, and updated versions will be posted on our website. Continued use of our website after updates indicates your acceptance of the revised policy.
